Vegan Chocolate Cake Recipe

This delicious vegan chocolate cake is moist, rich, and completely plant-based. Perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients: 2 cups flour, 1 cup cocoa powder, 1 cup plant milk, 1/2 cup coconut oil, 1 cup sugar.

Mix all dry ingredients in a large bowl. Combine wet ingredients separately, then fold together. Pour into a greased pan and bake at 180°C for 35 minutes.
