Understanding Machine Learning: A Beginner's Guide

Machine learning is a subset of artificial intelligence that enables systems to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed.

There are three main types of machine learning: supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and reinforcement learning. Each has its own use cases and advantages.

Supervised learning involves training a model on labeled data, where the desired output is known. Common algorithms include linear regression and neural networks.

The field has seen rapid growth in recent years thanks to increased computational power and the availability of large datasets.
